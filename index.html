About Nemanja

I left a career to
build something real.

I used to work in IT - coaching corporate companies on how to work better. I had stability, a good income, and a path that made sense on paper. But something inside me knew there was more.

I left it all. I travelled the world, learned from spiritual teachers, visited holy places, and explored healing traditions across cultures. Over a decade, I completed numerous healing modalities and coaching trainings - not as a collection, but as a calling.

Today, through energy healing, Kundalini Yoga, and professional coaching, I've helped hundreds of people unlock their energy, release emotional blockages, and return to a life that feels genuinely theirs.

I work in English and Serbian, serving clients from Vienna and internationally - all online.
